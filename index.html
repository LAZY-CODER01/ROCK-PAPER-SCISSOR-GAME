**Rule:**

- Each player simultaneously chooses one of three options: rock, paper, or scissors.

- Rock beats scissors: If a player chooses rock and the other player chooses scissors, the player with rock wins because rock can crush scissors.

- Scissors beat paper: If a player chooses scissors and the other player chooses paper, the player with scissors wins because scissors can cut paper.

- Paper beats rock: If a player chooses paper and the other player chooses rock, the player with paper wins because paper can cover rock.

By understanding this rule, players can predict the outcomes of their choices and compete effectively in the game of Rock, Paper, Scissors.
